Calvin Klein Boys' Denim Shorts

Designed for boys who love to move, these denim shorts combine effortless style with all-day comfort. The classic 5-pocket construction and signature logo patch make them a perfect fit for any casual outfit.

Key Features

  • Signature logo patch for brand recognition – A subtle yet powerful way to showcase style and identity.
  • Relaxed fit design – Ensures comfort through long playtime and everyday activities.
  • 5-pocket style with traditional construction – Functional and timeless, ideal for outdoor adventures.
  • Zipper fly and button closure – Easy to wear and take off, adding convenience to every move.
  • Durable denim fabric – Built to last through outdoor play and rugged adventures.

Why Choose These Shorts?

Whether your child is playing in the park, heading to school, or just zipping around the house, these shorts are built to keep up.
