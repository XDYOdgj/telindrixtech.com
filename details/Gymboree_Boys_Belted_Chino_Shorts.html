Stylish Belled Chino Shorts for Boys and Toddlers

These Gymboree chino shorts are designed for curious little boys and toddlers who love to move, play, and stay on trend. With a vibrant tropical paradise design, complete with a playful flamingo theme, they're the perfect choice for beach days, family outings, or any adventure in between. The lightweight 98% cotton twill fabric guarantees softness and breathability, while the 2% spandex adds just the right amount of stretch for comfort during all-day activities.

Features That Make a Difference

  • Tropical paradise design with flamingo theme
  • 98% cotton twill fabric for soft comfort
  • Adjustable striped belt for a custom fit
  • Hook-and-bar button closure with zip fly

Why Choose These Shorts?

Great for active kids, these shorts are built to keep up with every move, whether it's playdates, park adventures, or summer trips.
