Calvin Klein Boys' Pull-on Fleece Jogger Shorts

Elevate your little one's casual look with these versatile and stylish jogger shorts. Designed for active boys, they offer the perfect balance of comfort and durability, making them ideal for all-day wear.

Style Meets Functionality

These shorts feature a bold large vertical logo graphic on the left leg, adding a touch of sporty flair to any outfit. The relaxed fit sits below the waist and falls just below the knee, ensuring ease of movement during play or daily routines.
